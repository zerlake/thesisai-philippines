## How to Use the New Features

### ReviewerAiToolkit (Reviewer AI Toolkit)

1. **Select text** in your document
2. **Click Advanced Options** to customize:
   - Writing Tone (Formal/Professional/Conversational/Academic)
   - Target Audience (Academic/Professional/General/Expert)
   - Complexity Level (Advanced/Intermediate/Beginner)
3. **Choose operation**: Improve, Summarize, Paraphrase, or Get Feedback
4. **View result** in the preview area
5. **Regenerate** to get variations
6. **Insert** directly to document or **Copy** to clipboard

### Paraphrasing Tool

1. **Paste or type** your text
2. **Select mode** from the dropdown
3. **Read the description** of what the mode does
4. **Click "Rewrite Text"**
5. **View character counts** for both versions
6. **Use controls**: Copy, Save as Draft, Preview
7. **Undo** previous rewrites if needed

## Support

### Common Questions

**Q: Why does it take 10 seconds?**  
A: Puter AI service processes remotely. This includes latency + processing.

**Q: Can I use different AI providers?**  
A: Currently Puter only. Future upgrades will support OpenRouter, Claude, etc.

**Q: Does it work offline?**  
A: No, requires internet connection to Puter AI service.

**Q: Can I adjust the prompts?**  
A: Not yet, but this is planned for future versions.

**Q: Does it save my text?**  
A: Only if you explicitly save as draft. By default, text is not saved.
