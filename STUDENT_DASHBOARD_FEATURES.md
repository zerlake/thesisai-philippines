# ThesisAI Student Dashboard - Comprehensive Features Guide

## Overview

The ThesisAI Student Dashboard is a comprehensive workspace designed to assist students throughout their thesis or dissertation journey. It provides a structured approach to thesis writing with AI-powered tools, progress tracking, and milestone management.

## Main Dashboard Components

### 1. Welcome Section
- Personalized greeting with student's name
- Action plan and project overview display

### 2. What's Next Card
- **Purpose**: Displays the next recommended action for the student
- **Features**:
  - Critical, high, and normal priority tasks
  - Direct links to relevant tools or tasks
  - Advisor feedback notifications
  - Milestone deadline alerts

### 3. Thesis Checklist
- **Purpose**: Step-by-step guide from topic to thesis submission
- **Features**:
  - Four phases: Getting Started, Structuring Your Project, Writing It Up, and Finishing Touches
  - Interactive checklist with progress tracking
  - Direct links to relevant tools
  - Progress percentage visualization
  - Confetti celebration when all items are completed
  - Drag-to-complete functionality

### 4. Statistics Cards
- **Total Documents**: Shows the number of documents created
- **Total Word Count**: Displays overall word count with recent activity (last 7 days)
- **Average Words per Document**: Shows the average length of documents

### 5. Quick Access Tools (16+ Tools)
- **Topic Idea Generator**: Brainstorm and develop research topics
- **Outline Generator**: Structure thesis chapters and sections
- **Research Helper**: Find and evaluate academic papers
- **Methodology Helper**: Design research methods and approach
- **Results Helper**: Present research findings effectively
- **Conclusion Helper**: Summarize research and provide recommendations
- **Presentation Maker**: Create presentation slides
- **Flashcards**: Study and memorize key concepts
- **Originality Check**: Scan for plagiarism and ensure originality
- **Reference Manager**: Manage citations and bibliography
- **Document Analysis**: Analyze PDFs and documents
- **Research Article Analyzer**: Extract structured information from research articles
- **Literature Review Collaboration**: Collaborate on literature reviews
- **Variable Mapping Tool**: Map research variables and relationships
- **University Format Checker**: Check compliance with format requirements
- **Research Problem Identifier**: Identify research problems and gaps

### 6. Progress Tracking Widgets

#### Recent Activity Chart
- **Purpose**: Visualizes word count of the last 5 documents
- **Features**: Bar chart showing document progress over time

#### Session Goal Card
- **Purpose**: Set and track daily writing goals
- **Features**:
  - Custom goal setting (e.g., "Write 300 words")
  - Completion tracking with checkbox
  - Editable goals

#### Writing Streak Card
- **Purpose**: Motivates consistent writing habits
- **Features**: Tracks consecutive days of document activity

#### My Milestones Card
- **Purpose**: Manages thesis deadlines and milestones
- **Features**:
  - Advisor-assigned deadlines
  - Completion status tracking
  - Overdue alerts
  - Visual indicators for completed milestones

### 7. Progress Milestones Component
- **Purpose**: Self-managed thesis progress tracking
- **Features**:
  - Predefined milestones (Proposal Defense, Chapter Drafts, Final Defense, etc.)
  - Completion tracking with checkboxes
  - Visual progress indicators

### 8. Advisor Feedback Card
- **Purpose**: Shows documents requiring revision
- **Features**:
  - Direct links to documents needing changes
  - Visual notification for revision requests

### 9. Contextual Action Buttons
- **Purpose**: Provides contextually relevant actions
- **Features**:
  - "Go to Next Task" direct link
  - "Continue Writing" to most recent document
  - "Start 15-min Session" with focus timer

### 10. Wellbeing Check-in Widget
- **Purpose**: Monitors student wellbeing during thesis process
- **Features**:
  - Mood selection (Feeling Good, Neutral, Needing Support)
  - Emotional check-in to promote mental health

### 11. Support Cards
#### User Guide Card
- **Purpose**: Provides access to comprehensive user guidance
- **Features**: Direct link to detailed user documentation

#### Testimonial Submission Card
- **Purpose**: Allows students to provide feedback
- **Features**:
  - Text area for feedback submission
  - Submission confirmation

### 12. Additional Features

#### Welcome Modal
- **Purpose**: Introduces new users to the platform
- **Features**: Onboarding experience for first-time users

#### Bug Report Alert
- **Purpose**: Provides a mechanism for reporting issues
- **Features**: Easy-to-access bug reporting functionality

#### Upgrade Prompt Card
- **Purpose**: Promotes premium features to free users
- **Features**:
  - Prompts for Pro plan upgrade
  - Highlights premium benefits (unlimited documents, advanced AI, etc.)

#### Data Management Plan (DMP) Form
- **Purpose**: Helps students plan research data handling
- **Features**:
  - Compliance and ethics section
  - Data handling and security section
  - Sharing and archiving section
  - IRB approval tracking
  - Data privacy compliance
  - Storage and backup planning
  - Advisor review submission

#### Student Detail View
- **Purpose**: Comprehensive student profile management
- **Features**:
  - Document status tracking (Draft, Submitted, Approved, Needs Revision)
  - Milestone tracking
  - Checklist completion overview

#### Student Progress Overview Chart
- **Purpose**: Visualizes progress distribution
- **Features**: Bar chart showing percentage of thesis completion

#### Critic Billing History
- **Purpose**: Tracks financial transactions with critics
- **Features**:
  - Historical review fee tracking
  - Payment status indicators
  - Document and critic association

## Dashboard Customization

The dashboard supports configurable widgets that can be toggled based on user preferences, allowing each student to customize their view based on their most relevant needs during different phases of their thesis journey.

## Integration Points

- **Authentication**: Secure login and profile management
- **Supabase Backend**: Real-time data synchronization and storage
- **AI Tools**: Integrated AI assistance for writing and research
- **Document Editor**: Seamless integration with document creation tools
- **Advisor/Critic Communication**: Direct communication channels for feedback

## Access

The student dashboard is accessible at `/dashboard` and is the primary interface for students to manage their thesis projects.
