# Advisor and Critic Guide: Interconnected Dashboard System

## Overview
Welcome to the enhanced ThesisAI dashboard system! This guide explains how the interconnected dashboard system works, allowing you to collaborate effectively with your assigned students and see real-time updates from all connected parties.

## What's New
- **Real-time Updates**: Changes made by students appear instantly on your dashboard
- **Synchronized Data**: All information across student, advisor, and critic dashboards stays in sync
- **Collaborative Workflow**: Seamless interaction between all parties involved in thesis completion

## For Advisors: How to Use the System

### 1. Viewing Student Information
- Your dashboard automatically shows all students assigned to you
- Student progress, document status, and activity are updated in real-time
- Any changes students make to their profiles or thesis progress are immediately visible

### 2. Document Review Process
- When students submit documents for review, they appear in your queue
- As you review and update document statuses (e.g., "needs revision", "approved"), these changes appear immediately for the student
- Students can see the status of their documents and any feedback you provide

### 3. Providing Feedback
- When you submit feedback on student documents, it appears immediately in the student's dashboard
- Students are notified of your feedback and can respond or make necessary changes
- All feedback history is maintained and can be referenced during the thesis process

### 4. Progress Tracking
- You can monitor individual student progress and thesis completion stages
- If a student updates their progress in their dashboard, your view updates immediately
- Milestone completions by students appear in real-time on your dashboard

## For Critics: How to Use the System

### 1. Managing Student Assignments
- Your dashboard shows all students assigned to you for certification/evaluation
- Student thesis status and completion progress are updated in real-time
- Any changes students make to their work appear immediately in your view

### 2. Review and Certification Process
- Submitted documents from your assigned students appear in your review queue
- As you update certification statuses, students see these changes immediately
- Your feedback and evaluation appear directly in the student dashboard

### 3. Communication Updates
- When you provide certification feedback, students are notified instantly
- Students can respond to your feedback, and these conversations update in real-time
- Any changes to certification requirements or status are immediately visible

## Real-Time Synchronization Features

### Student to Advisor/Critic Updates
- **Document Submission**: When students submit documents, they appear immediately in your queue
- **Progress Updates**: Student progress markers update on your dashboard as they complete milestones
- **Status Changes**: If students mark tasks as completed, your view updates immediately

### Advisor/Critic to Student Updates
- **Feedback Delivery**: Your feedback appears instantly in the student's dashboard
- **Document Review Status**: Students see their document status updates (needs revision, approved) in real-time
- **Milestone Approvals**: When you approve milestones, students see this reflected immediately

### Cross-Party Updates
- If both advisor and critic are involved, updates from one automatically appear for the other
- Document status changes (from either advisor or critic) update for all parties
- Progress tracking is synchronized across all dashboards

## Best Practices

### For Advisors:
- Check your dashboard regularly for new student submissions
- Provide timely feedback to keep the thesis process moving
- Use the progress tracking features to identify students needing extra support
- Monitor document status changes to stay on top of your review tasks

### For Critics:
- Regularly review your certification queue for new submissions
- Communicate with both students and advisors when necessary
- Use the real-time updates to keep the certification process efficient
- Track student progress to ensure they're meeting requirements

## Troubleshooting

### If Real-Time Updates Don't Appear:
1. Refresh your browser to ensure you have the latest connection
2. Check your internet connection
3. Verify that the student has properly saved their changes in their dashboard

### Communication Issues:
- Ensure students have your correct contact information in their profiles
- Confirm that notification settings are enabled for both parties
- Use the in-platform messaging system for important updates

## Support
For technical issues with the dashboard synchronization, contact technical support. For questions about using the new features, consult the help section within your dashboard.
