## Tool Descriptions

### Generate
- **When:** Phase 1 & 3
- **Creates:** Introductions, sections, paragraphs from scratch
- **Use if:** You have a blank page or need to start a new section

### Grammar
- **When:** Phase 3 & 4 (Phase 4 has advanced customization)
- **Improves:** Clarity, grammar, academic tone
- **Use if:** You've written content and want to polish it

### Summarize
- **When:** Phase 2, 3, & 4
- **Creates:** Shorter versions of your text
- **Use if:** You need to condense long sections or research

### Paraphrase
- **When:** Phase 3 & 4
- **Options:**
  - **Paraphrase:** Reword using different vocabulary
  - **Make More Formal:** Elevate tone for academic audience
  - **Simplify:** Make easier to understand
  - **Expand:** Add more detail and elaboration
- **Use if:** You want to rewrite content without plagiarizing

### Advanced Options â­
- **When:** Phase 4 ONLY
- **Customizes:**
  - Writing Tone (Formal, Professional, Conversational, Academic)
  - Target Audience (Academic, Professional, General, Expert)
  - Complexity Level (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced)
- **Use before:** Final polishing, creating abstracts, presenting to specific audience

### Submit for Review â­
- **When:** Phase 4 ONLY
- **Sends:** Your final thesis to your assigned advisor
- **Creates:** Submission record and triggers advisor notification
- **Use when:** Your draft is complete and you're ready for feedback
