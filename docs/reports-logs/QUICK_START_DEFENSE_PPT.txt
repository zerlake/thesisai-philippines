‚ùì FAQ
================================================================================

Q: Can I load a sample and edit it?
A: Yes! Load sample, then click Edit Slides tab and customize.

Q: Can I save my edited sample?
A: Yes, edits are saved to localStorage. Custom edits in Edit tab.

Q: Can I present without fullscreen?
A: Yes, Present tab works in windowed mode. Ctrl+F for fullscreen.

Q: How long does a sample take to go through?
A: Proposal: ~15 min, Final: ~25 min based on slide timings

Q: Can I add more slides?
A: Yes, click Edit Slides tab, then [+ Add Slide] button

Q: Do I need to memorize keyboard shortcuts?
A: No, UI buttons are always available. Keyboard is optional.

Q: Can I export my presentation?
A: Currently edit and present. Future: PDF/video export planned.

Q: Are speaker notes included?
A: Yes! All samples have detailed speaker notes for each slide.

Q: What if I make mistakes during practice?
A: Reload the sample - no changes are saved permanently.
