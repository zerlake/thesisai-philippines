# Chapter 1: Introduction

## 1.1 Background of the Study
The rise of artificial intelligence (AI) has led to significant advancements in various fields, including academic research. This study explores the impact of AI-powered tools on the thesis writing process for undergraduate students.

## 1.2 Statement of the Problem
Many students struggle with the complexities of academic writing, including literature reviews, data analysis, and citation management. This research investigates whether AI tools can effectively alleviate these challenges.
